# Shortcuts(for source viewer)

- I ,O:- In and Out point of a media
- Shift + I:- Go to Inpoint
- '+' + "Time":-To go "time" ahead
- Alt + "select" :- To overwrite the link of video and audio to select only video or audio
- N:- Toggle Snapping
- F:- To reveal the original source of a clip in timeline
- Ctrl + B :- To split a clip at playerhead
