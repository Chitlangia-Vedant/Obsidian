# Revenue
Identify the contracts with a customer -> Identify the performance obligations in the contract -> Determine the transaction price -> Allocate the transaction price to the performance obligation in the contract -> Recognize revenue when (or as) the entity satisfies a performance obligation

Revenue is recognized as the control is passed, either over time or at a point in time 
## Factors
- The entity has a present right to payment for the asset.
- The customer has legal right to the asset
- The entity has transferred physical possession of the asset
- The customer has significant risk & reward related to the ownership of the asset
- The customer has accepted the asset
## An entity revenue over time if one of the following criteria is met
- The customers simultaneously receive & consume all the benefits provided by the entity as the entity performed
- The entity's performance creates or enhances an asset that customer control as the assets is created
- The entity's performance does not an asset with an alternative use to the entity & entity has an enforceable right to payment for performance completed to date.
