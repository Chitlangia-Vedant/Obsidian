# Accounting(Element of FS)
- Balance Sheet
	1. Assets
	2. Liability
	3. Equity
- Income Statement
	4. Income
	5. Expense

Financial Statement are written records that convey the business activities & the financial performance of a company.

- Balance sheet: Position of a company at a point in time.
- Income sheet: Profitable for a particular period.
- Cash flow: Cash inflow & outflow for a particular period.

## Assets 
A resource controlled by the entity as a result of post event & from which future economic benefits are expected to flow to the entity
### Include
- Plant, Machinery & Equipment
- Account receivable
- Cash
- Inventories
- Investments
## Labiality
Present obligation of the entity arising from past events, the settlement of which is expected to result in an out from the entity of resources embodying economic benefits.
### Include
Loan
Creditors
Tax payable
Unearned revenue
Salary payment
## Equity
It is the residual interest in the assets of the entity after deducting all its liabilities.
### Include
- Owners contribution
- Retained earning
- Comprehensive income
## Income
It is increases in economic benefits during the accounting period in the form of inflows or enhancements of assets or decreases of liabilities that result in increases in equity, other than those relating to contribution from equity participants.

### Include
- Sales of goods & services
- Gain on sale of assets 
- Royalty fees
- Interests and dividend received
- Rent received

# Income Statement Format
**Revenue:** Income generated from day to day activities of the company
**Cost of good sold:** Direct cost related to the revenue
**Gross Profit:** Profit after deducting direct cost from the revenue
**Operating Expenses:** Other cost related to operation

EBITDA
	Profit after deducting all operation cost from the revenue
	Depreciation & Amortization: Charge related to the fixed assets used in the business
EBIT/PBIT
	Profit after deducting all cost except interest & tax
	Interest: Cost for borrowed funds
EBT
	Profit Before tax
	Tax: Cooperate Tax
PAT -> Net Income -> Profit After Tax
