# Supply Chain 
**Consist of a network of organization.**

Supply chain management involve coordinate of activities across 4 stage 

Raw material → Manufacturing → Wholesaler/Retailer → Customer

It is very useful in monitoring how efficiently and effectively a company is able to drive its primary goal of providing goods or services.
