# What is Financial Model?
A numerical representation → of some (or all) financial aspect → of an entity operation

It is a data driven planning tool for making a financial decision.
# How is it made?
Spread sheet or Dedicated Financial Software
# Who makes FM?
- Investment Banker 
- Equity Researcher
- Financial planning advisor
- Private Equity & Venture Capital proff.
- Hedge fund manager
# Why are FM made?
Made to assist in decision making & planning.
# What does FM includes?
1. **Business valuation**: Used to estimate the attractiveness of a business or compare to its peer.
2. **Stock Valuation**
3. **Project evaluation/Strategic planning**
# What a "good" model should aim for?
1. Realistic 
2. Error Free
3. Flexible
4. Easy to use
5. Self-explanatory
